====== 31. Namita si Jesus sa Babun Lanêm ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-01.jpg}}

Then Jesus told the disciples to get into the boat and sail to the other side of the lake while he dismissed the crowd. After Jesus had sent the crowd away, he went up on a mountainside to pray. Jesus was there all alone, and he prayed until late at night.

Matapos mangaral ni Jesus, sinabi niya sa mga apostol na sumakay na sila sa bangka at pumunta na sa kabilang pampang ng lawa habang pinapauwi niya ang mga tao. Pagkatapos niyang pauwiin ang mga tao, umakyat siya ng bundok para magdasal. Mag-isa lang si Jesus doon at nagdasal siya hanggang lumalim ang gabi.


Pamakayari, inpasakay Jesus sa bangka ya mani tagasunul na, buy inpaunan lumipay sa Betsaida buy inpauli nay kalak-an. Sên nakabitay nay mani tawu, sinumukpa si Jesus sa gisay bung-uy amên manalangin.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-02.jpg}}


Meanwhile, the disciples were rowing their boat, but by late in the night they had only reached the middle of the lake. They were rowing with great difficulty because the wind was blowing hard against them. 

Samantala nasa gitna pa lang sila ng lawa kahit gabing gabi na. Nahihirapan na ang mga apostol na magsagwan dahil sa mga alon at hangin na sumasalubong sa kanila.

Kaban idi pun sa bunak dagat ya bangkan mani tagasunul na, magkasakit ya mani tagasunul na sa pagsagwan, gawan salungat sila sa masikan na bagyu. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-03.jpg}}


Then Jesus finished praying and went to the disciples. He walked on top of the water across the lake toward their boat!

Tapos ng magdasal si Jesus kaya pinuntahan niya ang mga apostol. Lumakad siya sa ibabaw ng tubig papunta sa bangka nila!

Inlakun Jesus ya mani tagasunul na un mamita sa babun lanêm.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-04.jpg}}

The disciples were very afraid when they saw Jesus, because they thought they were seeing a ghost. Jesus knew that they were afraid, so he called out to them and said, "Do not be afraid. It is I!"

Takot na takot ang mga apostol nang makita nila si Jesus dahil akala nila, multo ang nakikita nila. Alam ni Jesus na natatakot sila kaya pasigaw niyang nasabi, “Huwag kayong matakot, Ako ito!”

Sên nakit mani tagasunul la dilag mamita sa babun lanêm, kinêdêsan sila gawan sa sadyay limu la. Kabay nakaangaw silan paradi, “Anitu!” Tandan Jesus ya malimu sila. Kabay nagbêg ya, “Agana kaw malimu, ta sikuy ati!” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-05.jpg}}

Then Peter said to Jesus, "Master, if it is you, command me to come to you on the water." Jesus told Peter, "Come!"

Sinabi ni Peter kay Jesus, “Panginoon, kung ikaw talaga iyan, papuntahin mo ako diyan.” Sinabi ni Jesus kay Peter “Halika!”

Sinabin Pedru kan Jesus, “Panginuun, nu sabay sikay abiin, ipalaku muku baysên un mamita êt sa babun lanêm.”

Nakitbay si Jesus, “Kaksaw ka!”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-06.jpg}}

So, Peter got out of the boat and started walking to Jesus on the surface of the water. But after walking a short distance, he turned his eyes away from Jesus and began to look at the waves and to feel the strong wind.

Kaya humakbang si Peter palabas sa bangka at lumakad sa ibabaw ng tubig papunta kay Jesus. Nakakailang hakbang pa lang siya, lumingon si Peter at naramdaman ang malakas na hangin at nakita ang malalakas na alon.

Kabay nagtabuy si Pedru sa bangka buy namita sa babun lanêm papakun kan Jesus. Nuwa nalimwan na gawan nakit nay malakas sa angin. Kabay a nayna nakitan si Jesus.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-07.jpg}}

Then Peter became afraid and began to sink in the water. He cried out, "Master, save me!" Jesus reached out right away and grabbed him. Then he said to Peter, "You man of little faith, why did you doubt?"

Natakot si Peter at nagsimulang lumubog sa tubig. Napasigaw siya, “Panginoon, iligtas mo ako!” Lumapit agad si Jesus at sinagip si Peter. Sinabi niya kay Peter, “Nagkulang ka ng pananampalataya, bakit ka nag-alinlangan?”

Gawan baysên, nalimwan Pedru buy pakanawa yan nilêbêg. Nan-angaw ya, “Panginuun, iligtas muku!”  Tambêng yan dinwang Jesus buy sinabi na, “Nabêlêng yay panampalataya mu ta! Buy siyan magmitalisa yay nakem mu ta?” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-08.jpg}}

When Peter and Jesus got into the boat, the wind immediately stopped blowing and the water became calm. The disciples were amazed. They worshiped Jesus, saying to him, "Truly, you are the Son of God."

Nang makabalik na si Peter at Jesus sa bangka, tumigil kaagad ang pag-ihip ng malakas na hangin at pumayapa na ang tubig. Namangha ang mga apostol. Pinuri nila si Jesus at sinabi, “Tunay ngang ikaw ang Anak ng Diyos.”


Pamakasakay lan luwa sa bangka, tinunggên na malakas sa angin. Buy inulimên yan mani tagasunul na ya idi sa bangka buy sinabi, “Pêtêg yabay ya sikay Anak Bapan Namalyari!” 

//A Bible story from: Matthew 14:22-33; Mark 6:45-52; John 6:16-21//
