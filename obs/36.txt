====== 36. Nagbayuy lupan Jesus ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-01.jpg}}

One day, Jesus took three of his disciples, Peter, James, and John with him. (The disciple named John was not the same person who baptized Jesus.) They went up on a high mountain by themselves to pray.

Isang araw isinama ni Jesus ang tatlo sa mga apostol niyang sina Peter, James at John. (Ang John na ito ay hindi ang John na nagbawtismo kay Jesus). Umakyat sila sa mataas na bundok para magdasal.

Gisay allu, pikilakun Jesus ya tatluy tagasunul na, si Pedru, buy ya mipatêl la silan Santiagu buy si Juan. (Si Juan na pantukuyun baydi alwan si Juan na namawtismu kan Jesus). Kay kakapad lay sinumukpa sa matag-ay ya bung-uy amên manalangin. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-02.jpg}}

As Jesus was praying, his face became as bright as the sun and his clothes became as white as light, whiter than anyone on earth could make them.

Habang nagdarasal si Jesus naging kasing liwanag ng araw ang mukha niya at ang damit niya. Wala pang ganitong liwanag ang nakitang mas hihigit pa sa liwanag na ito.

Kaban manalangin si Jesus, sinumawang nga lupa nan nanuwad mamut buy ya yaming na, pinupumutin nanuwad sawang. [Wala pang ganitong liwanag ang nakitang mas hihigit pa sa liwanag na ito.]

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-03.jpg}}

Then Moses and the prophet Elijah appeared. These men had lived on the earth hundreds of years before this. They talked with Jesus about his death, which would soon happen in Jerusalem.

Pagkatapos biglang lumitaw sina Moses at Elijah. Nabuhay ang dalawang lalaking ito sa mundo daan-daang taon na ang nakalipas bago pa man ang pangyayaring ito. Nakipag-usap silang dalawa kay Jesus tungkol sa kamatayan niya na malapit nang mangyari sa Jerusalem.

Mamakun ni nakitan [biglang lumitaw] lay makisabi si Moises buy si Elias kan Jesus. Sabitun nabyay sila baydi sa babun luta, niêtêng silan tidinalan na tawun bayu malyari ya ati. [Nabuhay ang dalawang lalaking ito sa mundo daan-daang taon na ang nakalipas bago pa man ang pangyayaring ito.] Pisasabyan lay tungkul sa kamatyan Jesus ya marani yan malyari sa Jerusalem. [sounds like they are talking about Jesus' death and Jesus is just listening in?]

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-04.jpg}}

As Moses and Elijah were talking with Jesus, Peter said to Jesus, "It is good for us to be here. Let us make three shelters, one for you, one for Moses, and one for Elijah." Peter did not know what he was saying.

Habang kinakausap nila Moses at Elijah si Jesus, sinabi ni Peter kay Jesus, “Maganda na narito tayo. Gumawa tayo ng masisilungan para sa inyong tatlo, isa para sa inyo, isa para kay Moses at isa para kay Elijah.” Hindi pinag-isipan ni Peter kung ano ang mga nasabi niya.

Kaban pakisabyan Moises buy si Elias si Jesus, sinabin Pedru kan Jesus, “Panginuun, mangêd da manugêl [narito/idi] kay baydi. Nu labay mu, manyag gakun [kitamun] tatluy sawung: gisa kamu, gisa kan Moises buy gisa êt kan Elias.” Sinabin Pedruy abiin gawan a na tanda nu sabêt ta pansabin na.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-05.jpg}}

As Peter was talking, a bright cloud came down and surrounded them and a voice from the cloud said, "This is my Son whom I love. I am pleased with him. Listen to him." The three disciples were terrified and fell on the ground.


Habang nagsasalita pa si Peter bumaba ang isang maliwanang na ulap at pinalibutan sila. May boses galing sa ulap na nagsalita at sinabi, “Siya ang pinakamamahal kong Anak. Nalulugod ako sa kanya. Pakinggan niyo siya.” Napadapa sa lupa ang tatlong apostol dahil sa takot.

Kaban magsabi pun si Pedru, natugpawan buy napalitêngan silan mamakasilêw wa ulap. Naubat sa ulap pa magsabin [May boses galing sa ulap na nagsalita at sinabi], “Ati ya kakaidwan kun Anak ya lubus kapapaidu kangku. Mallêngê kaw kana!” Gawan sa limun mani tagasunul nipaglukub sila sa luta.[switch the clause?]


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-06.jpg}}

Then Jesus touched them and said, "Do not be afraid. Get up." When they looked around, the only one still there was Jesus.

Hinawakan sila ni Jesus at sinabing, “Huwag kayong matakot. Tumayo kayo”. Pagtingin nila sa paligid wala na silang nakitang iba doon kundi si Jesus.


Tinalan silan Jesus buy sinabi na kalla, “Midêng kaw, gana kaw malimu.” [switch the clause?] Sên nangêlêw sila sa palitêng la, kay si Jesus tanay nakitan la. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-07.jpg}}

Jesus and the three disciples went back down the mountain. Then Jesus said to them, "Do not tell anyone yet about what happened here. I will soon die and then come back to life. After that, you may tell people."


Bumaba na ng bundok sina Jesus at ang tatlo niyang apostol. Sinabi ni Jesus sa kanila, “Huwag muna ninyong ipagsabi sa iba ang nangyari dito. Malapit na akong mamatay pero mabubuhay akong muli. Pagkatapos maaari na ninyong ikuwento sa mga tao ang nangyari.”

Kaban mallumunsan si Jesus buy mani tagasunul na sa bung-uy, [Lumunsan si Jesus buy mani tagasunul na sa bung-uy. Sinabin] sinabin Jesus kallay, “Gana yu sabin agyan kan sabêt man na nakitan yu anggan asê manguman mabyay ya Anak Tawuy ubat sa langit.  Nu nalyari yay sinabi ku, malyari yinan ikuwentu.[Malapit na akong mamatay pero mabubuhay akong muli. Pagkatapos maaari na ninyong ikuwento sa mga tao ang nangyari.]” 

//A Bible story from: Matthew 17:1-9; Mark 9:2-8; Luke 9:28-36//
