====== 28. Ya Liyaki ya Mabandi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-01.jpg}}

One day, a rich young ruler came up to Jesus and asked him, "Good teacher, what must I do to have eternal life?" Jesus said to him, "Why do you call me 'good'? There is only one who is good, and that is God. But if you want to have eternal life, obey God's laws."

Gisay allu, dilag gisay liyaki ya nagdani kan Jesus buy namatang, “Mangêd da Manuru, sabêt ta sêpat kun daygên amên mabiyan nakun biyay ya ayin katganan?” 

Nakitbay si Jesus, “Awta binêg muku yan mangêd ta? Ayin kaatag ga mangêd nun a kay si Bapan Namalyari. Nu labay mun mabiyan kan biyay ya ayin katganan, sunulun muy mani utus Bapan Namalyari.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-02.jpg}}

"Which ones do I need to obey?" he asked. Jesus replied, "Do not murder. Do not commit adultery. Do not steal. Do not lie. Honor your father and mother, and love your neighbor as you love yourself."

Namatang manguman na liyaki, “Sabêt sa mani utus sa ati?”  

Nakitbay si Jesus, “Agana ka mamati, agana ka makikanayun, agana ka manakaw, agana ka êt papêtêg sa ayin kapêtêgan, galangên muy bapa buy indu mu, buy idun muy kaparisu mu kaparisun pangidu mu sa sarili mu.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-03.jpg}}

But the young man said, "I have obeyed all these laws ever since I was a boy. What do I still need to do in order to live forever?" Jesus looked at him and loved him.

Nuwa sinabin liyaki, “Sinunul kinay kaganawan abiin ubat pun sabitun anak kaku. Sabêt pun êt ta kaylangan kun daygên?” Inêlêw yan Jesus gawan [and] kaidwan naya.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-04.jpg}}

Jesus answered, "If you want to be perfect, then go and sell everything you own and give the money to the poor, and you will have treasure in heaven. Then come and follow me."

Nakitbay si Jesus, “Nu labay mun magpakapêtêg ka sa arapan Bapan Namalyari, isaliw muy kaganawan pibandiyan mu. Buy si naabli muy pera, idin mu sa mani kalulu. Sa kapamilatan abaytu, mabiyan kan pibandiyan sa langit. Pamakayari, mag-udung ka buy sumunul ka kangku.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-05.jpg}}

When the young man heard what Jesus said, he became very sad, because he was very rich and did not want to give away all the things he possessed. He turned and went away from Jesus.

Nuwa sên nalêngên liyaki abaytu, namita yan malungkut, gawan malakêy pibandiyan na buy a na labay matasan yan pibandiyan. Kabay nagpabukut ya kan Jesus buy inlakwanan naya. [revised]

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-06.jpg}}

Then Jesus said to his disciples, "It is extremely hard for rich people to enter into the kingdom of God! Yes, it is easier for a camel to go through the eye of a needle than for a rich man to enter the kingdom of God."

Kabay sinabin Jesus sa mani tagasunul na, “Masakit miawyun sa pag-arian Bapan Namalyari ya tawuy mabandi. Ta nu a misun na kamelyu sa lubut karayum, lakwas sabay ya mabandi, a ya malyarin miawyun sa mani pag-arian Bapan Namalyari.[from Luke - Ya mani tawuy mabandi, masakit silan misun sa pag-arin Bapan Namalyari. Tambêng pun misun na gisay kamelyu sa lubut kadayum, kaysa magpasakup pa mabandi sa pag-arin Bapan Namalyari.]” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-07.jpg}}

When the disciples heard what Jesus said, they were shocked and said, "Then who can be saved?"

Nakaupapas sa mani tagasunul na. Kabay namatang sila, “Nu parabaydu, sisabêt ta miligtas?”


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-08.jpg}}

Jesus looked at the disciples and said, "With people this is impossible, but with God, all things are possible."

Nangêlêw si Jesus kalla buy sinabi na, “Asê madyag tawuy iligtas nay sarili na, nuwa madyag Bapan Namalyari ya kaganawan.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-09.jpg}}

Peter said to Jesus, "We have left everything and followed you. What will be our reward?"

Sinabin Pedru kan Jesus, “Parasaantu kay yaman na tagasunul mu? Inlakwanan yan nay kaganawan amên sumunul kamu. Sabêt ta gantimpala yan?”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-10.jpg}}

Jesus answered, "Everyone who has left houses, brothers, sisters, father, mother, children, or property for my sake, will receive 100 times more and will also receive eternal life. But many who are first will be last, and many who are last will be first."

Nakitbay si Jesus, “Pakalêng-ên yuy ati. Nu sisabêt man na mallakwan bali' na, asawa na, mapatêl na, mangatwa na, buy maanak na gawan sa pag-arin Bapan Namalyari, mabiyan yan igit pun kaysa sa inlakwanan na sa panawun amêsên. Buy sa panawun na lumatêng, makatanggap ya êt biyay ya ayin katganan.” 

//A Bible story from: Matthew 19:16-30; Mark 10:17-31; Luke 18:18-30//
